Types of Data :- 1. Numerical
                 2. Categorical

Types of Analysis:- 1.univariate
                    2.bivariate & multivariate


1. univariate Analysis

Categorical data - Countplot, PieChart
Numerical data - Histogram , Displot , Boxplot


2. bivariate/multivariate Analysis 

Numerical-Numerical  = scatterplot
Numerical-Categorical = Barplot
Numerical-Categorical = Boxplot
Numerical-Categorical = Displot
Categorical-Categorical = Heatmap
Categorical-Categorical = Clustermap
Categorical-Categorical = Heatmap
Numerical-Numerical = LinePlot
